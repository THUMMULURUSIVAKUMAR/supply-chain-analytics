## Problem Statement
DataCo Global is experiencing losses due to late deliveries, demand fluctuations, and inefficient inventory management across multiple departments and regions. This project analyzes customer demand patterns, identifies delivery delay drivers, forecasts future product demand, and provides data-driven operational recommendations.

## Project Structure
- `01_eda.ipynb` — Exploratory Data Analysis
- `02_demand_forecast.ipynb` — ML Demand Forecasting Model
- `03_inventory_analysis.ipynb` — Inventory Optimization and EOQ
- `04_ops_recommendations.ipynb` — Operations Strategy and KPI Dashboard
- `Supply_Chain_Insights_Report.pdf` — Full Business Report

## Dataset
- Source: DataCo Smart Supply Chain Dataset (Kaggle)
- Records: 180,000+ orders
- Features: 50+ columns

## Key Results
- Random Forest Demand Forecast: R2 Score = 0.92
- Identified top 3 high-demand products for priority restocking
- Found 50%+ late delivery risk in Standard Class shipping
- Calculated EOQ for 100+ products to minimize inventory costs

## Tech Stack
- Python, Pandas, NumPy
- Scikit-learn (Random Forest, Linear Regression)
- Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook (Anaconda)

## Business Recommendations
1. Switch high-value orders to First Class shipping
2. Implement EOQ-based restocking for top products
3. Cap discounts at 20% to protect profit margins
4. Focus budget on Latin America and Europe markets
5. Deploy forecasting model into monthly procurement planning
